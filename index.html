What Citadene is.

A privately held South African software company. We don't take outside capital, we don't sell vapourware, and we don't quote on integrations we can't deliver. Every product we ship has to stand on its own commercially.

Our mandate

Every operation in this country runs on a mix of paper, WhatsApp, spreadsheets and legacy systems that were good twenty years ago. Citadene builds the operational software that closes those gaps — the kind of software that gets used at 4am at a depot, not just demoed in a boardroom. We work in narrow problem spaces (fleet, tolling) where the gap between "what the system says" and "what actually happened" is where all the money is.

How we work

Small team, high standards, no agency model. Software is written by the people accountable for it. Pilots are paid and time-boxed. Customers own their data. We default to plain English in contracts and documentation. POPIA-aligned across every product.
